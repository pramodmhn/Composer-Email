Welcome to our newsletter
Our vision is that Aboriginal Victorians will have equality of opportunity and outcomes with non-Aboriginal Victorian.

All Victorians will recognise, respect and celebrate the contribution of Aboriginal Victorians to Victorian community life

We work to achieve this vision by :
  • Building a framework to support and develop leadership and capacity building in Victoria's many Aboriginal communities.
  • Establishing a representative structure for all Aboriginal Victorians to voice their opinion.
Regards,



John Smith
