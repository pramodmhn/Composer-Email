Workforce Reporting
 
SMR Service, together with the Ministry of Health, Workforce Planning & Performance Unit, added another set of reports that focuses on monthly measures relating to Service Level Agreements (SLA). 

This SLA reporting is under the Workforce Profile Dashboards and contains the following metrics:

• Monthly FTE
• Monthly Variance to Budget – Pay Amount
• Premium Staff Usage - % of Total FTE
• Sick Leave Hours per FTE
• Premium Staff Usage – YTD Variance
• Annual Leave Liability – Instances for Payroll

Forecast Planning Tool
 
The SMR Service, together with the Ministry of Health Finance Division and Health Agencies, released Oracle Hyperion Planning application that assists in the monthly forecast/projection estimation.

The key benefits of having the forecast planning application are:
Standardisation of the accounts used in forecasting
Pre-population of actual, and budget data used for forecast projection
Standardised business rules on how forecasts are completed
Reduce the amount of time required to update the forecast leaving more time for analysis.

Customer feedback

"This tool was designed with the intention of gaining consistency in forecasting across Health entities, utilising data already in the system to provide automated forecasting models and make the monthly forecasting process more efficient for all users." Steven Black, Associate Director of Treasury & Capital Reporting, Ministry of Health
